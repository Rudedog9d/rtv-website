Our Mission

The Red Team Village is focused on training the art of critical thinking, collaboration, and strategy in offensive security. The RTV brings together information security professionals to share new tactics and techniques in offensive security. Attendees may spend time in introductory workshops or challenge themselves in an immersive Capture the Flag competition to put their newly obtained skills to the test.
